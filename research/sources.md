## Expert Selection Criteria

These 10 experts were selected based on the following criteria — NOT by Googling "top SEO experts" and picking the first results:

1. **Practitioner-first, not pundit-first**: They publish based on real experiments, client data, and workflow tests — not just aggregated advice from other blogs.
2. **AI-native focus**: Their content specifically addresses how to use AI tools (LLMs, automation, APIs) within a structured content production workflow.
3. **Recency**: All sources are active in 2025–2026 and are adapting their frameworks to the post-AI-Overview, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) era.
4. **Signal strength**: Prioritized experts who drive real measurable outcomes (traffic case studies, tool results, rank changes) over thought leaders who only theorize.
5. **Diversity of angle**: Combined technical SEO (schema, entity, crawling), content workflow (AI production, editing, fact-checking), and strategic (GEO, E-E-A-T, topical authority) perspectives to capture the full stack of AI-SEO.
